The Mystery Black Box

First and foremost, we started off the year as guessing what was in this mystery black box. No matter how much water you added into it, the black box would either spew out more water, or even less water, or sometimes just a couple of drops! While we were guessing on how to approach this mystery black box, we had to try to figure out a pattern within the box, whilst not being able to see the inside.

After doing some serious considerations, we started building straight away! My group ended up doing pretty well, yet nevertheless, we never could find out what was truly inside of that mystery black box.

Screenprinting, Video, and Genetics

After this subject, we moved on to screenprinting, and actually made some edits of our project, which overviewed the whole process. My screen printing design was about not giving into evil desires.

After this, we moved onto genetics and finding out about our DNA, who we connect with the closest out of all of our classmates. One of the people whom I connected with the most was Evan Ortiz, due to the fact that we are both Italian. We swabbed our cheeks with a toothpick and put it inside of a machine which determines all of that. We also recorded and edited some videos of this entire process.
